About Me

I am a first year PhD student at the School of Interactive Computing in Georgia Institute of Technology working with Dr. Alan Ritter.

I received by B.S. in Computer Science and B.A. in Linguistics from University of Washington. During my time at UW, I was fortunate to work with Dr. Luke Zettlemoyer and Terra Blevins.

Research Interest

I work on Natural Language Processing (NLP). Specifically, I am interested in understanding the performance of language models and how to make them more robust. I have always wanted to incorporate more linguistic knowledge into NLP too.
